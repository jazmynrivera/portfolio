far therapeutic arts

far therapeutic arts is an organization based in birmingham, mi, focused on providing therapy in arts, recreation and leisure for community members with special needs. their last website update was in 2014, and was in need of a face lift. my senior capstone team and i updated the overall look of the site, as well as added new features to improve user experience.

we began with user research, interviewing current clients of FAR in addition to potential clients who were unfamiliar with the site. we developed user personas and user journeys, to help us get an idea of what users came to the website to achieve.

after gathering data from our participants, we began wireframing our design changes. this included rearranging the navigation bar, adding a footer, and creating a donation page. we used adobe xd to implement our designs.

using our wireframes, we developed a high fidelity, interactive prototype with figma that conveyed our suggested design changes.

adue web team

in september of 2018, i began working for the multidisciplinary design program (mdp) for the university of michigan. i was a developer on their website, doing maintenance throughout the school year and summer. in september of 2019, eight other departments in the college of engineering approached mdp, asking for updated websites of their own.

i led a team of 12 developers, and we worked with the associate dean for undergraduate education to provide their websites. i taught the team the research and design process, how to use wordpress, and how to fully implement webpages using html/css/js.

in addition to supervising the team, i was lead developer for the honors engineering department. i worked with my client from scratch to develop a new look and feel for the site through iterative design. hover over to see the old site.
